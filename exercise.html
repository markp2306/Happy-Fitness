Bench Press (Placeholder)

A compound exercise for the chest, shoulders, and triceps, performed while lying on a bench.

1

Lie flat on your back on a bench and grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width.

2

Lower the bar slowly to your chest while keeping your elbows at a 45-degree angle.

3

Push the bar back up to the starting position with explosive power.

4

Maintain core tightness throughout the entire movement for stability.
